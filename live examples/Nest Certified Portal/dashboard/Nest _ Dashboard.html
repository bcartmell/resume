November 19, 2013

CO protection for your customers.

When your customers have both a Nest Thermostat and Nest Protect, they have extra protection against carbon monoxide poisoning. If Nest Protect's CO alarm goes off, the Nest Thermostat will automatically turn off fossil fuel heating systems without needing additional wiring. Some states already require you to put in a CO alarm when installing a new furnace. Nest Protect and the Nest Thermostat can be easily connected through the Nest app to save time and keep your customers safe. Remember, if your customers love their Nest Thermostat, they'll probably love Nest Protect. And you can install smoke alarms year-round.

November 19, 2013

Software update for Nest Thermostat and app.

We're updating both the Nest Thermostat and the Nest app this week. New features include

  • Quiet Time is a brand new 4.0 feature that allows homeowners to set times when noisy stand-alone humidifiers and dehumidifiers won't turn on, regardless of the humidity level. That helps preserve the most precious thing in many homes: the kids' nap time.
  • Heat Pump Balance improvements include more efficient Balanced and Comfort settings and pre-heating using multiple stages of heat.
  • Our thermostat can now activate second and third stage heat more efficiently to avoid using auxiliary heat while maintaining comfort.
  • Nest will do a better job switching between fuel types in dual fuel systems to protect heating systems and provide more consistent heat.
